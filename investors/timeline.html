Investing in a platform

A Global Series as a Platform

What differ Label Drinkers from a regular tech startup is of course the nature of being a global series format and not a regular online service.
With a focus on global reach and once signed for distribution, we will directly have an initial reach to Millions of potential viewers. With a single Global distributor, or several regional of the same, reach adds tens of millions of potential viewers.

Popular Chef's Table have an estimated figure of 20M viewers on a regular basis. With a Global reach the brand Label Drinkers have the potential to act as a major platform for third party brand exposures while simultaneously becoming a launch platform for new products or services adding upon our brand.

Early investors have the possiblities to either support the scale up process, earning a quick ROI, or maintain ownership and make use of the platform for future product releases. This seed investment will help us extend our sales capabilities while adding extensive reach on a global market for IP sales. A target date is already set for our presence on the MIPTV show in Cannes early April, where we have secured an exclusive and selected on stage apperance.
