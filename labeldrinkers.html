In Label Drinkers the viewer will be shown the magical world of Champagne [SE01] where producers, together with our recognised hosts discuss everything from wine making processes down to nerdy details, satisfying the most prestigious taster or enthusiast.

But wait, that's what all series about beverages do!!

We've done it differently, by gathering some of the worlds leading experts to host and reveal what people really would like to watch...

The consuming side and their behaviours.

Storyline

Two hosts with world class expertise and award-wining resumes in the gastronomical topic revealing luxurious fine dining and behaviours around the world. Secrets from celebrities party life to hidden and mysterious sides of the rich and famous, sports, First Class flights to Formula One races and a lot lot more, including famous guests. Simultaneously this series will uncover stories and reveal myths connected to each episode story line.

Why Champagne?

The first seasons have a clear Champagne focus to highlight unique elements we've newer heard of or seen before, we reveal the secrets of the region and the uniqueness of the only place in the world allowed to use the name Champagne.

Wine Producers

We reveal the untold storys from famous champagne houses and se the hidden treasures that goes way beyond traditional tasting.
The qwest to find, reveal or bust myths take our hosts around the globe. Meeting everything from a selection of wine makers and chef de caves to the sometimes complete opposite in their customers or consumers as we call them here.

 

Consumption Behaviours

Besides the producers point of view we also explore the consumer behaviours around this beverage, all from crazy poolpartys, insane tabs to bottle delivery we cant imagine.

Audience

The series to be distributed for a Global audience, produced in a new agile workflow to gain speed and efficency.
