In Label Drinkers the viewer will be shown the magical world of Champagne (Season 1) where producers, together with our recognised hosts discuss everything from wine making processes down to nerdy details, satisfying the most prestigious taster or enthusiast.

But wait, that's what all series about beverages do!!

We've done it differently, by gathering some of the worlds leading experts to host and reveal what people really would like to watch...

The consuming side and their behaviours.

From luxurious fine dining around the world to celebrities, party life, hidden and mysterious sides of rich and famous with Yachts, Private Jets and First Class flights to Formula One races and a lot lot more, including famous guests.

Simultaneously this series will uncover and reveal myths connected to each episodes story line.
