Label Drinkers is a cinematic gastronomy and beverage show that explores everything beyond food artsy and kitchen creations.

Our hosts explore the global and eccentric consumption behaviours, the fascinating industry that supports it, and the variety of myths and legends surrounding a range of famous beverages.

A journey where they meet not only passionate experts but celebrities, winemakers, chefs and other characters, all with unique stories to tell.

Label Drinkers fusion of education and entertainment brings the viewers on a journey where they will appreciate the best of both worlds.

Label Drinkers will engage the audience in a tour de force around the globe with it's exciting and humorous style on the quest to explore the world of champagne. Our hosts will expose untold stories and busting myths, challenged by the best experts in their field, creating unexpected, controversial and thrilling situations - meeting producers, personalities, stars and celebrities in legendary venues and locations around the globe.

Label Drinkers explore the ultimate Champagne experience in the travel-, hospitality- and gastronomical industry, while simultaneously covering historical revelations and trends that over centuries have cemented Champagne as the no.1 beverage of them all.

Experts on all levels

"I am triggered by the fact that this is not a traditional food or wine show but something completely different. With speed and variety, this show captures the viewers at the same time as it teaches and hopefully becomes rewarding for the audience in my favorite subject Champagne." - Richard Juhlin

 

Not just do we have our fantastic hosts in front of the camera, we also have the world-renowned Richard Juhlin to assist our hosts in selected elements trough out the entire series.

Behind the cameras we have handpicked a team of sommelier world champions, decanter world wine awards judges, rare fine-wine experts and more to assure the viewers will get the absolute crème de la crème of knowledge, expertise and high quality discussions all over the series.

Dynamic elements

 

Challenges and events are introduced as gripping and brave add-ons to the traditional story telling. Each episode contains non-repeating element order that gives the audience a unique and dynamic experience for each episode.

We follow our charismatic hosts vibrant yet contrasting views in the global hunt for unique locations, producer stories, amazing venues, high ranking restaurants and exclusive clubs that are highly regarded and associated with some of the most recognised prestige beverage brands in the world.

Made for a global audience

With our global focus, the show will be in English and naturally also targeting global VOD distributors.

We will take the audience on a dream like escapade revealing stories and facts covering many cultures around the globe; following the glamour of the rich and famous to the untold stories by the producers and the people behind the creation processes.

Like when our hosts are challenged to hunt down the worlds most expensive champagne - a journey that will take us from Formula One races to three-star restaurants with their chefs and sommeliers to the cellars of rare and hidden wine collections.

Cinematic expression

We have handpicked the production crew specifically for the cinematic expression we develop for this series.

The crew we have brought together have extensive and collective experience from Endemol Shine, Warner Bros, SVT, SF, Sony pictures, Digital Domain, Technicolor, TV4-Group, Viasat to name a few.
